4. **Proper Business Model Representation**: Accurately describes vendor-reimbursed model, transparently addresses commission concerns in Objection 2, and positions advisory as cost-saving rather than cost-adding—aligning with UPSTACK's core differentiation.

5. **Research Integration Framework**: Properly structures findings across layers and traces recommendations back to research (legal operations evolution, regulatory environment, IT operations constraints)—shows understanding of how insights were derived.

These fabrications violate the Brand & Model Alignment standards and render the playbook unsuitable for production use—they would damage credibility upon discovery by prospects and expose the company to reputation risk.

1. Remove all three instance of the "$2,000 monthly circuits" example and replace with verified retention/NPS proof points
2. Remove fabricated "$180K savings" case study from Touch 5; replace with statement of typical outcomes
