## SERVICE DELIVERY MODEL COMPARISON

### Vendor Models

**Direct Sales + Post-Implementation Support**
- Fee: Embedded in product pricing / professional services contracts
- Duration: Ongoing (recurring software subscriptions)
- Focus: Upsell, retention, customer success

**Marketplace / Channel Private Offers**
- Fee: 3% (cloud marketplaces) to 20% (hardware on AWS)
- Duration: Per-transaction
- Focus: Volume, consumption

### Advisor Models

**Vendor-Reimbursed (UPSTACK-Like)**
- Fee: Commission from vendor (not paid by buyer)
- Duration: Ongoing (lifecycle management)
- Focus: Long-term relationship, vendor accountability

**Professional Services (Hours-Based)**
- Fee: Charged directly to buyer
- Duration: Project-based
- Focus: Specific evaluation or implementation
