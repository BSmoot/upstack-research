Brokers are transactional in nature, focused on their own business rather than the client. Brokers tend to approach partnerships with the question "what's in it for me?" in mind. Moreover, brokers are hyper-focused on the commission, completing a lease or sale without the intention to necessarily repeat business with a client.

**Key insight**: UPSTACK must never be framed as a "broker" in buyer conversations, even if the distribution model resembles one.
