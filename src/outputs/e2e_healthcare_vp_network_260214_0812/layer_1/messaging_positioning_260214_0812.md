The revenue foundation of a technology advisor business is its commissions, which the provider pays on a monthly basis for the lifetime of the customer, with an "evergreen" stream of recurring commissions to the agent that brought it the customer. **UPSTACK's advantage**: Ongoing revenue only when the client stays satisfied, creating accountability over years, not at point of sale.
