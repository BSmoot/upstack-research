## **OBJECTION HANDLING**

### **Objection 1: "We don't have budget for advisory services"**
**Response:**
"I understand budget constraints—that's exactly why our model works for healthcare organizations. You pay nothing. Vendors fund our advisory fees the same way they pay their own sales teams. In most cases, our clients get better pricing and terms than going direct because we bring market intelligence from thousands of completed engagements. One client saved $180,000 annually just from vendor contract optimization—more than enough to justify the relationship even if there was a cost."

### **Objection 2: "We have good relationships with our current vendors"**
**Response:**
"That's actually ideal. Good vendor relationships are exactly what we help you optimize and protect. The question isn't whether your vendors are good—it's whether you're getting optimal value, performance, and compliance from those relationships over time. Our 99.8% client retention rate proves we enhance existing vendor relationships rather than disrupt them. We often help clients negotiate better terms with vendors they want to keep."

### **Objection 3: "We handle vendor management internally"**
**Response:**
"Most healthcare IT teams do handle vendor management—between everything else you're managing. The challenge isn't capability, it's capacity. Your team fights fires while managing strategic initiatives. Meanwhile, each vendor optimizes for their own interests. We provide ongoing advocacy and market intelligence that your internal team simply doesn't have time to develop. Think of us as extending your team's capabilities, not replacing them."

### **Objection 4: "How do we know your recommendations aren't biased toward vendors who pay you?"**
**Response:**
"Great question—transparency is fundamental to our model. First, we're paid by whichever vendor you select, not by specific vendors trying to influence our recommendations. Second, our 99.8% retention rate proves our recommendations work—we only succeed when our clients succeed long-term. Third, we disclose our compensation model upfront and provide detailed evaluation criteria for every recommendation. Happy to show you exactly how our evaluation process works."

### **Objection 5: "We don't have time for another vendor relationship"**
**Response:**
"That's the point—we reduce your vendor management burden, not add to it. Instead of managing 20+ vendor relationships, you have one point of contact that manages all of them on your behalf. Our clients typically see a 60% reduction in time spent on vendor-related issues. We handle renewals, performance monitoring, compliance tracking, and escalations. You get more vendor oversight with less time investment."
